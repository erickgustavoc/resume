Erick Gustavo Chuquichambi

About

Hello! I am a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Psychology at the University of the Balearic Islands (UIB). I am a member of the Human Cognition and Evolution group (EvoCog) and the Active Cognition, Embodiment & Environment Lab (ACE2Lab). I did my PhD in Human Cognition and Evolution and completed my postdoc at the UIB. I have been a visiting researcher at the Liverpool Hope University (2021), University of Padova (2022), BI Norwegian Business School (2023–2024), and University of Toronto (2024).

Research

My research focuses on the psychological and neural bases of visual preferences and multisensory experiences. I am also interested in social perception, data science, and implicit cognition.
